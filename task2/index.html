Why Web Development Matters

Six things I've learned about why web dev is actually important

👁️

First Impressions Happen Fast

People judge a website in seconds. A clean, working layout tells visitors your project is worth their time. A broken one does the opposite, even if the content is great.

🧭

Good UX Keeps Users Around

A website that's hard to use gets abandoned. When navigation is clear and pages load quickly, users actually stick around and do what you want them to do.
